Lakeside Middle School

Cumming, GA

The Lakeside MS project was a renovation of the existing mechanical system and the addition of a new ground up classroom building for Forsyth County Schools.

Project Details

This project included complete demolition and rebuild of the main mechanical room with pumps, boilers, cooling tower, and heat exchanger. It also included the replacement and addtion of the water source heat pumps, DOAS and ERU systems, roof top units, split sytems, BMS controls, and new duct suystems.
